About Us | BasketballStarsGames.github.io

Last Updated: May 22, 2025

BasketballStarsGames is a browser-based gaming platform offering free and unblocked HTML5 games, with a primary focus on basketball-themed titles like the popular Basketball Stars. Our platform is designed to be fast, accessible, and enjoyable for players of all ages.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of casual browser gaming as a tool for relaxation, entertainment, and social connection. Our goal is to make high-quality games accessible to everyone—whether at school, at work, or at home—without the need for downloads, registration, or payments.

What We Offer

  • 100% free HTML5-based basketball games
  • Mobile and desktop compatibility
  • No login or account creation required
  • Unblocked and lightweight performance for quick play sessions
  • User-friendly interface and fast load times

Ad Monetization

To keep our services free and sustainable, we display ads through Google AdSense. These ads are family-friendly and adhere to Google’s strict content policies. We never display popup ads or autoplay videos with sound.

Analytics and Improvements

We use Google Analytics to measure traffic and improve the performance of our site. The data collected is anonymous and used solely for internal optimization. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Community Interaction

We welcome respectful and constructive comments on our website. Any user-submitted content must comply with our Terms and Conditions. Offensive, harmful, or spam content will be removed.

Commitment to Privacy

We value user privacy and are fully committed to maintaining transparency. We do not collect personally identifiable information and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA regulations.

Contact

If you have questions, feedback, or legal inquiries (including copyright concerns), please use the contact form or links in the footer of the website. We strive to respond within 72 business hours.

Thank You

We’re thrilled to share our love for basketball games with the global gaming community. Thank you for visiting and playing on BasketballStarsGames!
